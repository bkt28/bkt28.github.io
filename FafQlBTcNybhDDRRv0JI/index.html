About Us

ducry-finance sa is a family business founded in 2004 by Dominique Ducry. He was joined by his wife, Marianne Ducry, a few years later. Since 2016 she has been involved full-time for Cantin SA, a company owned by Mr. Ducry, where she is now the CEO.

In 2017 Caroline Englund, Mr. Ducry’s step-daughter, joined the consulting firm and created the Impact Consulting sector of ducry-finance sa.

Impact Consulting is a type of strategic consulting dedicated to Non-Profit Organizations, Social Enterprises and other entities that aim to have a social impact. For-Profit Companies can also benefit from this type of service, when they decide to create or improve on a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) plan.

Services

Impact Consulting

Is it for me?

Yes, if you are a Non-Profit Organization (NPO), a Social Enterprise (SE) or any other entity that works toward a social impact, and can relate to one of these statements:

We need to increase our efficiency! We should do some rebranding! It’s time for change! What’s our actual impact? We are implementing a new program!

What can we do for you?

Depending on your needs and objectives we will do one, some of all the tasks below:

Foundations Check
  • What are your theories of change, mission and vision?
  • Who are your clients/beneficiaries?
  • How do you measure your results and impact(s)?
  • What are your strategies?
  • What is your financial model?
Optimization
  • Define or redefine your foundations upon what is needed.
Establishment
  • New strategies corresponding to your needs (up to your quadruple bottom line).
  • New programs/projects
Implementation
  • We will guide you throughout the changes agreed on.

Is it for me?

Yes, if you are a For-Profit Company and can relate to one of these statements:

We want to do some good! We want to be associate to a more positive image! It’s time for some positive change! We are implementing a new CSR program!

What can we do for you?

Depending on your needs and objectives we will do one, some of all the tasks below:

Work beyond your bottom line
  • Have you heard about double, triple and quadruple bottom line?
  • How do you feel about adding a social and/or an ecological impact and maybe even a more meaningful purpose to your company?
  • Did you know that a positive image could help you retain your employees and improve your sales?
Creating a full CSR project
  • There are many ways of creating a CSR project, we will work with you to choose the one that fits you best.
Internal Image
  • What do your employees feel and need, did you ever ask them?
  • Do you have a strong culture? A sense of belonging?
Implementation
  • We will guide you throughout the implementation of the changes agreed on.
